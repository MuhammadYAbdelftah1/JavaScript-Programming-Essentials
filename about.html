About Muhammad Abd El-Fattah Tours

Founded in 2020, Muhammad Abd El-Fattah Tours has been helping travelers discover their perfect destinations around the world.

Our team of experienced travel enthusiasts curates personalized recommendations based on your preferences, ensuring unforgettable experiences.

Our team consists of experienced travelers, local guides, and travel technology experts who have visited over 100 countries combined. We're passionate about helping others discover the perfect destinations that match their unique travel style.

Our Mission

At Muhammad Abd El-Fattah Tours, we believe that travel should be transformative and tailored to each individual's desires.

At Muhammad Abd El-Fattah Tours, our mission is to transform how people discover and experience destinations around the world. We believe that travel has the power to broaden perspectives, create meaningful connections, and enrich lives.

We're committed to providing personalized travel recommendations that go beyond the typical tourist attractions, helping you discover hidden gems and authentic experiences that align with your interests and preferences.
